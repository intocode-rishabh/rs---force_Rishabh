How to Find a Service

A step-by-step guide to get the help you need from local professionals.

1

Select Your Service Category

Choose the type of professional you need from our 6 service categories:

🔧 Plumber 📚 Tutor ⚡ Electrician 🛵 Delivery Agent 🩺 Doctor 🛠️ Others
2

Browse Service Providers

See cards of local providers who have listed themselves. Each card shows their name, profession, location, about, and hourly rate. Filter by maximum price to find the best match for your budget.

3

Post a Request (Optional)

Alternatively, post a request describing what you need — just like a tweet. Relevant service providers will see your post and reach out to connect with you directly.

📝 Twitter-style post 🎯 Category auto-tagged 👁️ Visible to providers
4

Connect & Chat

Click on any provider's card to view their full profile. Message them directly on NeighbourHub, or reach out via their WhatsApp, Instagram, or Twitter. No middleman, no commission.
